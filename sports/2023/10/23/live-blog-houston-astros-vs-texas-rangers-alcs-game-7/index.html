RECAP: Houston Astros fall to Texas Rangers 11-4 in Game 7 of ALCS; Rangers advance to World Series

A trip to the World Series was on the line for the Houston Astros and the Texas Rangers as both teams faced each other one last time Monday night.

Cristian Javier started for the Astros in Game 7, while Max Scherzer started for the Rangers.

The Rangers will advance to the 2023 World Series and will face either the Phillies or the Diamondbacks.

KPRC 2 Sports Director Randy McIlvoy, Sports Reporter Ari Alexander, and Digital Producer Ana Gonzalez brought you the biggest plays and moments from the game.
