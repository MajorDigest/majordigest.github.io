High school girls basketball rankings Jan. 2, 2024: Bay Area News Group Top 20

There is no doubt at the top of the Bay Area News Group girls basketball rankings. 

Archbishop Mitty tightened its vice-grip on the Bay Area (and country’s) No. 1 spot when it defeated top-ranked Long Island Lutheran at the Nike Tournament of Champions, and then beat three other powerhouses in Portland over the holiday break. 

San Ramon Valley remains locked at No. 2, and Carondelet, Bishop O’Dowd, Acalanes and Piedmont battle in the next tier of excellent East Bay teams. 

Mitty’s West Catholic Athletic League rivals St. Ignatius and Sacred Heart Cathedral are joined by surprising upstart Archbishop Riordan. 

But near the bottom of the list, two of the Bay Area’s premier programs, Oakland Tech and Saleisan, are still ranked despite having losing records. 

Why is this?

Both have challenged themselves against top-notch competition, which could pay off as they move into their league schedules. 

With that said, on to the rankings.
