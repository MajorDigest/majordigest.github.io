Ludacris to perform at third annual 'Dinner on the Diamond' event

SAN DIEGO (FOX 5/KUSI) -- Rapper Ludacris is set to perform on stage at Petco Park this summer, the San Diego Padres and Padres Foundation announced Wednesday.

The performance is part of the third annual Dinner on the Diamond event, presented by Sycuan Casino Resort, which is set to take place on Thursday, July 11 at 6 p.m.

The event features a gourmet dinner from the playing field with tables hosted by Padres players, coaches, executives and alumni, live entertainment by Ludacris and a live auction.

Table reservations for Dinner on the Diamond are open to the public. Tables hosted by alumni start at $15,000 and VIP tables hosted by players start at $25,000.

Proceeds from the event will go toward the Padres Foundation and the Peter Seidler Legacy Fund projects. The projects include working with underserved youth, health and wellness, education and those impacted by homelessness in the San Diego and Northern Baja communities.

More information on Dinner on the Diamond presented by Sycuan Casino Resort is available at www.padres.com/dinner.
