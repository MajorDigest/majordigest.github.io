Major downtown street closures begin Thursday night for upcoming NASCAR Chicago Street Race

CHICAGO — Major road closures take effect downtown beginning Thursday night and continuing through the upcoming NASCAR Chicago Street Race.

Road closures began on June 10 as set-up got underway for race weekend, which is Saturday and Sunday, July 6-7, in and around Grant Park.

But the major closures begin to take effect at 7 p.m. Thursday, when Jackson Drive will be closed between Michigan Avenue and Columbus Drive.

The next closures take effect on Friday. At 8 a.m., Balbo Drive will be closed from Columbus Dr. to DuSable Lake Shore Drive. At 7 p.m., the Balbo Dr. closure will extend west to Michigan Ave.

Further closures go into effect next week and will remain through race weekend, with select streets beginning to reopen on Monday, July 8.

All NASCAR-related demobilization activity is expected to conclude by the end of the day on Thursday, July 18.
