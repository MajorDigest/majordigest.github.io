New Orleans Saints wide receiver Chris Olave returns to Mission Hills for 3rd annual youth camp

SAN DIEGO (KUSI) - New Orleans Saints Wide Receiver and Mission Hills alum Chris Olave returns back home to host his 3rd annual youth camp at his alma mater.

When asked about the importance of keeping the tradition alive Olave says, "Giving back in the community, it's always one of my main reasons I was always one of these little kids looking up to these people.. it's a huge thing for me."

2017 Silver Pigskin Finalist will be entering his 3rd NFL season with the Saints, Olave surpassing the 1,000 yard mark both seasons. The WR1 looks to expand this fall, "I'm excited, it's a lot to look forward to. Just trying to win and get into the playoffs is the main thing but I'm just working to be my best for my teammates and uplift them."

Mission Hills Head Football Coach Chris Hauser reflecting on the full circle moment for his former player Olave saying, "He's still the Chris Olave that was on this campus back in '16/17 when he was here, he's just an incredible man and comes from a wonderful family, what a special event."

When asked about what qualities Coach Hauser saw in Olave back in his high school playing days, Hauser shares," His willing to just be a great teammate, I think we all recognized that he was super talented but he was so under the radar with that. He carried himself like he was one of the guys, which he was and he wanted to be that, he built friendships and relationships with the entire team."

The WR1 and the New Orleans Saints will begin their regular season September 8th against the Carolina Panthers.
