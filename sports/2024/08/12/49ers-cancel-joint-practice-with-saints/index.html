49ers cancel joint practice with Saints

IRVINE, Ca. (WGNO) —The Saints planned two joint practices with the San Francisco 49ers on Thursday, Aug. 15 and Friday, Aug 16 but the 49ers had to cancel sessions.

The Athletics' Matt Barrows first reported San Francisco had to pull out of the joint session due to a mounting number of injuries on the roster: citing 23 players missing the Niners' previous practice on Aug. 10.

Following the New Orleans' Aug. 11 session, head coach Dennis Allen said the team would implement practices similar to what the Saints planned to do with the 49ers.

New Orleans will face San Francisco on Sunday, Aug. 18, during week two of the preseason.
