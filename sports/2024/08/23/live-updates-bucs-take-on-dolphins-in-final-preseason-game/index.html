FINAL: Bucs 24, Dolphins 14

TAMPA, Fla. (WFLA) -- The Tampa Bay Buccaneers are taking on the Miami Dolphins at Raymond James Stadium in the final preseason game of the year.

It is the team's only home game this preseason, and is the final chance for some of the young players to prove why they should make the team. The Bucs will have to narrow the roster down to 53 players by Tuesday.

Kickoff is at 7:30 p.m. Catch the game on WFLA News Channel 8.
