Buccaneers defeat Commanders 37-20 in dominant season opener in Tampa

TAMPA, Fla. (WFLA) -- Football is finally back and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers were eager to set a precedent with back-to-back season opener victories.

After leading the Bucs to an upset over Kirk Cousins and the Minnesota Vikings on the road, starting quarterback and team captain Baker Mayfield is on the hunt for another dominant season -- especially under first-year offensive coordinator Liam Coen.
