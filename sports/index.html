Santa Cruz Warriors reveals head coach Nicholas Kerr’s 2024-25 staff

The Santa Cruz Warriors revealed second-year Head Coach Nicholas Kerr's staff for the 2024-25 NBA G League season on Tuesday.  Hilton Armstrong and Ben Stelzer have both been named assistant coaches. (Sentinel Staff Report, The Mercury News)

Jeff Ulbrich was a gritty player, a respected assistant and now takes over a struggling Jets team

Jeff Ulbrich was in the middle of putting together the game plan for the New York Jets' defense when he got the news that stunned him — and the rest of the NFL. Robert Saleh was out as the head coach. And Ulbrich was taking over for the rest of the season. (DENNIS WASZAK Jr., Associated Press, KGET 17, NBC)

Lynx return to finals with 88-77 win over Sun in Game 5 behind Collier's 27 points, 11 rebounds

MINNEAPOLIS (AP) — The Minnesota Lynx are three wins from a fifth championship, chasing a league record with an entirely different core from the previous dynasty. Napheesa Collier is fully in charge of this group. (DAVE CAMPBELL, Associated Press, KGET 17, NBC)

The NFL is reportedly investigating Pittsburgh Steelers wide receiver George Pickens for having an explicit message on his eye black against the Dallas Cowboys. (Fox News)

Dodgers turn to Brasier to start NLDS Game 4 vs Padres as they try to stave off elimination

SAN DIEGO (AP) — The Los Angeles Dodgers are going with reliever Ryan Brasier as their starter for Game 4 against the San Diego Padres on Wednesday night as they try to avoid elimination in their NL Division Series. Manager Dave Roberts said Tuesday that the Dodgers would go with a bullpen game. (BERNIE WILSON, Associated Press, PHL 17)

Bucs' 3-word message as Hurricane Milton set to devastate Florida

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers offered a three-word message to Florida residents as Hurricane Milton is set to devastate the state's western coast line. (Fox News)

Tatis homers again as the Padres beat the Dodgers 6-5 for a 2-1 NLDS lead

SAN DIEGO (AP) — Fernando Tatis Jr.'s towering two-run home run highlighted a six-run second inning, and the San Diego Padres held on to beat Shohei Ohtani and the rival Los Angeles Dodgers 6-5 on Tuesday night to take a 2-1 lead in a tense NL Division Series. (BERNIE WILSON, Associated Press, KGET 17, NBC)

2 teams suing NASCAR ask court to recognize them as chartered while antitrust case proceeds

CHARLOTTE, N.C. (AP) — The two teams suing NASCAR asked a judge for a preliminary injunction Wednesday so they can compete next season under the charter system they are challenging as their antitrust case moves through federal court. (JENNA FRYER, Associated Press, PHL 17)

Utah Hockey Club celebrates opening game with evening-long Delta Center plaza party

Just 172 days after it was announced Utah would be joining the National Hockey League, the Utah Hockey Club is set to play its first game in its inaugural season. (Derick Fox, ABC 4)

(AP) — Chicago Sky officials were light on specifics Wednesday when discussing their decision to fire former coach Teresa Weatherspoon after one season, saying there was no particular catalyst and that it was simply time to move on from the Naismith Hall of Famer. (ANDREW SELIGMAN, Associated Press, PHL 17)

Jets owner says firing of Saleh will bring 'new energy' to one of franchise's 'most talented teams'

(DENNIS WASZAK Jr., Associated Press, KGET 17, NBC)
