Boys Rugby: Cathedral Catholic 28, La Costa Canyon 17

The High School Boys Rugby Championship was held in Del Mar as the Cathedral Catholic Dons hosted the La Costa Canyon Mavericks. It was the Dons who came out victorious, defeated La Costa Canyon by a score of 28-17, to win their 5th straight Rugby Championship. (Chase Izidoro, Fox 5)

Real Madrid beats Atletico on penalties in Champions League. Arsenal, Villa, Dortmund also advance

Real Madrid beat Atletico Madrid in a Champions League penalty shootout — again — to keep its title defense alive Wednesday and advance to the quarterfinals.
