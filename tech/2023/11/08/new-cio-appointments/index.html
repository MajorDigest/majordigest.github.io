New US CIO appointments, November 2023

Movers & Shakers is where you can keep up with new CIO appointments and gain valuable insight into the job market and CIO hiring trends. As every company becomes a technology company, CEOs and corporate boards are seeking multi-dimensional CIOs with superior skills in technology, communications, business strategy, and digital innovation. The role is more challenging than ever before — but even more exciting and rewarding! If you have CIO job news to share, please email me!

Walgreens Boot Alliance names Neal Sample as CIO

Walgreens Boot Alliance

Walgreens Boots Alliance is an integrated healthcare, pharmacy and retail company. Sample held prior roles at Northwestern Mutual, Express Scripts, American Express and eBay. He earned a PhD and MS in computer science from Stanford University and an MS and BS in computer science from the University of Wyoming.

Modere appoints Guy Thier as CIO

Modere is a consumer products company that develops and markets clean health and wellness products through ecommerce and direct-to-consumer channels. Thier comes to Modere from Cutera, with prior positions at Arbonne International and Bally Total Fitness. He holds a BA in computer science from Lewis University and an executive MBA from Kellogg School of Management at Northwestern University.

Joseph R. Kowaleski promoted to CIO at Great American Insurance Group

Great American Insurance

Based in Cincinnati, the operations of Great American Insurance Group are engaged primarily in property and casualty insurance, focusing on specialized commercial products for businesses. Kowaleski joined the company in 2017 and most recently served as divisional SVP. He held a prior role at Hewlett Packard Enterprise. Kowaleski earned a BS in computer science from the University of Michigan and an MBA from Wayne State University.

Rajat Khanna joins Frontier Airlines as CIO

Frontier

Headquartered in Denver, Frontier operates aircraft serving destinations in the US, Caribbean, Mexico, and Central America. Khanna joins Frontier from Lowe’s Companies in Charlotte, NC, with a prior role at United Airlines. He earned a B.Tech from The NorthCap University and an MS in computer science from The University of Texas at Arlington.  

Rahul Goturi named CIO of TekniPlex

TekniPlex

Headquartered in Wayne, PA, TekniPlex provides medical device components and a multitude of material science solutions. Most recently, Goturi was with Buckman Laboratories, and prior to that, he held roles with W.R. Grace & Co. and Solutia. He earned an MS from the University of Missouri and an MBA from Washington University in St. Louis.

Neil Hampshire named CIO for Ocean Spray Cranberries

Ocean Spray

Founded in 1930 by three independent cranberry growers, Ocean Spray is now a cranberry cooperative, with more than 700 family farms across North and South America. Hampshire was previously at Subway and has held tech leadership roles with Reckitt, Wrigley, and Gillette. 

Superior Group of Cos. appointed Soo-Jin Behrstock as CIO

Superior Group

Superior Group of Companies, established in 1920, is a combination of companies that help customers with their brands by creating brand engagement experiences for their employees and customers. Previously, Behrstock held positions at King’s Hawaiian, AG Jeans, Koos Manufacturing, AppsPeri and Innowave Technology. She holds a BA from Portland State University.

Alcami announces Tariq Jamal as CIO

Alcami

Alcami provides solutions to the biotech and pharmaceutical industry across the entire life-cycle of products, from early development through commercial supply. Jamal came to Alcami from Lonza/Capsugel, and held previous roles at Novartis Consumer Health, Roche Pharmaceutical, and Genentech. He earned an MBA from Alliance Manchester Business School.

Jerry Sheehan named Salk Institute’s CIO

Salk Institute

Founded in 1960 the Salk Institute in La Jolla, CA, is an independent, non-profit, scientific research institute. Sheehan held prior roles at San Diego State University and Montana State University – Bozeman. He earned a BS and MS from Eastern Illinois University.

Autosled Appoints David Darden to CIO

Autosled

Autosled is a provider of digital technology solutions for automotive vehicle transport logistics. Prior to Autosled, Darden held positions at Palmetto, Big Fish Games, and Microsoft. He earned a BS in mathematics and computer science from University of Alabama at Birmingham.
