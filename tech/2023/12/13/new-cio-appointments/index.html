New US CIO appointments, December 2023

Movers & Shakers is where you can keep up with new CIO appointments and gain valuable insight into the job market and CIO hiring trends. As every company becomes a technology company, CEOs and corporate boards are seeking multi-dimensional CIOs with superior skills in technology, communications, business strategy, and digital innovation. The role is more challenging than ever before — but even more exciting and rewarding! If you have CIO job news to share, please email me!

AIG appoints Roshan Navagamuwa as CIO

AIG

American International Group is a global insurance organization. AIG member companies provide a wide range of property casualty insurance, life insurance, retirement solutions, and other financial services. Navagamuwa joins AIG from CVS Health and held a previous role at Aon. He earned a BS in computer science from Denison University.

Lori A. Meyer named CIO at First Interstate

First Interstate BancSystem, Inc. is a financial services holding company headquartered in Billings, Montana. Meyer started with the company in 1997 and has served in several leadership roles, most recently as interim CIO. She earned a BA from Montana State University.

Global Payments appoints Shannon Johnston as CIO

Global Payments

Headquartered in Georgia with approximately 27,000 team members worldwide, Global Payments is a payments technology company delivering software and services to customers. Johnston has served in several technology leadership roles at the company since joining in 2016, and held prior roles at Mspark, ShopVisible and Points of Light Institute.

Gil Salazar promoted to CIO at Stater Bros. Markets

Stater Bros. Markets

Stater Bros. was founded in 1936 and has become the largest privately-owned supermarket chain in Southern California. The company currently operates 171 supermarkets, with approximately 18,000 employees. Salazar most recently served as SVP of IT and had prior positions at Hyundai Capital America and Partners Consulting. He holds a BS from Liberty University and an MS from USC Marshall School of Business.

Wynn Resorts announces Dan Regalado as CIO – North America

Wynn

Wynn Resorts, Limited, owns and operates Wynn Las Vegas in Las Vegas, NV, Encore Boston Harbor in Everett, MA, as well as Wynn Macau and the Wynn Palace located in the Special Administrative Region of Macau in the People’s Republic of China. Regalado held prior roles at Banfield Pet Hospital and Walgreens Boot Alliance. He earned a BS in Computer Engineering from Mapúa University.

Lyft appoints Kevin Smith as CIO

Lyft

Lyft, headquartered in San Francisco, CA, is a provider of Transportation-as-a-Service. The company offers ride sharing, bike and scooter rental, access to autonomous vehicles, and provision of transportation options through Lyft platform and mobile-based applications. Smith held prior roles at Cloudera, Aurora, Stripe, and Twitch.

Virginia Tech announces Sharon P. Pitt as CIO

Virginia Tech

Virginia Tech, formally the Virginia Polytechnic Institute and State University, is a public land-grant research university with its main campus in Blacksburg, VA. Pitt holds a current role at Brown University and held prior roles at University of Delaware, Binghamton University in New York, and George Mason University. She earned an MS and BS from Virginia Tech.

Daren Hubbard named CIO for Princeton University

Princeton

Princeton University is a private Ivy Leagueresearch university in Princeton, NJ. Founded in 1746, Princeton is the fourth-oldest institution of higher education in the US. Hubbard comes to Princeton from Georgia Tech and had a lengthy career at Wayne State University. He earned an MLIS and MBA from Wayne State University.

United Wheels announces David Blum as CIO

United Wheels (UW) is a global holding company of outdoor brands including Huffy, Buzz E-Bikes, Niner Bikes, Batch Bicycles, Royce Union and VAAST Bikes. UWI brands are sold at thousands of retail locations shipping five million bicycles annually throughout the US and more than 40 countries. Prior to joining UW, Blum held positions at Hylant, W.W. Williams, and Highlights Corporation. He holds a BSC from Ohio University.

Santa Clara University names Rafael Espinosa III as CIO

Santa Clara University

Founded in 1851, Santa Clara University offers undergraduate programs in arts and sciences, business, and engineering, and graduate programs in six disciplines. The curriculum blends high-tech innovation with social consciousness grounded in the tradition of Jesuit and Catholic education. Espinosa joins the SCU community from California State University, Stanislaus. He had a prior role at the University of Chicago. Espinosa earned a BA from Northwestern University.

Courtney Lewis Starnes joins Kaleida Health as CIO

Kaleida Health

Kaleida Health is the largest healthcare provider in Western New York, serving the area’s eight counties. Starnes comes to Kaleida Health from Saint Luke’s Health System. Prior to that, she held positions at Children’s Mercy Hospital, Cerner Corporation. Starnes earned a BA from Missouri State University and an MBA from Rockhurst University.
