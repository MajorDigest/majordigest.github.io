A big Defense cybersecurity requirement for contractors moves closer to reality

The long awaited final rule is out, on a Defense Department cybersecurity program. Not yet in effect, the Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification program rule is now at the White House for review. So you might as well get used to the idea. Joining the Federal Drive with an update, attorney Eric Crucius of Holland and Knight.
