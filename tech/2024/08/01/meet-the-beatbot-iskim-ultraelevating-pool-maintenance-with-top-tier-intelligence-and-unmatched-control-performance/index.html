Meet the Beatbot iSkim Ultra—elevating pool maintenance with top-tier intelligence and unmatched control performance

Our daily lives have become surrounded by tech products. From smartwatches to smart vacuums and everything in between, the tech we live with gives us time back in our days to do the things we enjoy with the people we love. There’s one area of the home where the jump to smart technology has been lacking, however. Where are all the smart pools? A swimming pool can be a luxurious addition to your home, but the maintenance and cleaning of one can certainly dull its sparkle. Equipped with an array of smart features, the Beatbot iSkim Ultra can help bring that luxurious feeling back to your pool by keeping it clean and clear with minimal effort. No other pool cleaner has ever been this smart and efficient.  

Introducing the world’s first robotic pool skimmer with ultra cleaning performance 

Imagine a gust of wind sweeping through, scattering leaves, twigs, and insects across your pool. Especially during windy days, a freshly cleaned pool can become cluttered with debris in less than 24 hours. This frequent cleanup is a daunting task, often requiring you to roll up your sleeves or call your pool maintenance service again.

Enter the Beatbot iSkim Ultra, the latest innovation from the premium pool cleaning robot brand Beatbot. This solar-powered robotic pool skimmer is specifically designed to address the high-frequency cleaning needs of pools surrounded by dense trees or located in other environments where debris easily accumulates. Equipped with 7 motors, 20 high-precision sensors, including pioneering tri-ultrasonic technology and a high-performance six-axis IMU, the iSkim Ultra intelligently senses and analyzes the pool environment. Using Beatbot's advanced AI algorithms, it optimizes the cleaning path for maximum efficiency and coverage.

The iSkim Ultra's intelligent features are complemented by its unique brush system, which includes Beatbot's innovative dual-side brushes and an extra-long front roller brush. The dual-side brushes of the Beatbot iSkim Ultra work like synchronized swimmers, seamlessly guiding water flow inward and herding debris toward the center. As the debris is gathered, the extra-long front roller brush devours it, feeding the collected debris into an ultra-large 9L filter basket. This ingenious system ensures that debris near the sides isn’t pushed away but is instead captured efficiently, leaving the pool impeccably clean with every pass. Notably, this system also enhances the iSkim Ultra's ability to remove the debris along the edges and corners effectively.

The ultra-large filter basket doesn't just boast impressive capacity; it also features an anti-spill hatch at the debris entrance, ensuring that once debris is captured, it stays inside. After completing its cleaning cycle, the iSkim Ultra automatically returns to the poolside, floating on the surface and waiting for you to retrieve it easily and clean the filter.

Are you thinking these highly automated features are enough to save you from the tedious and time-consuming task of pool cleaning? Beatbot has thought of even more to cater to user needs. In addition to superior cleaning efficiency and coverage, the Beatbot iSkim Ultra is equipped with an exceptionally large battery, a 24W solar panel, and the industry-first SolarTrack™ light energy tracking technology, supporting ultra-long cleaning sessions around the clock. As Beatbot iSkim Ultra's secret weapon, the SolarTrack™ light energy tracking technology automatically activates when the battery is low and sunlight is uneven or insufficient, allowing it to maximize energy capture even on cloudy days for extended operation. Additionally, a magnetic wireless charger is included with Beatbot iSkim Ultra, providing a safer and more convenient option for recharging.

Are you frustrated with constant leaf and debris buildup, feeling disheartened when your pool remains dirty shortly after your pool service leaves? Considering calling your pool guy more frequently with more costs added up? Now, this is no longer a problem to worry about. With Beatbot iSkim Ultra, you have a tireless helper that guards your pool 24/7!

Innovative tech for ultra control performance 

The Beatbot iSkim Ultra exemplifies cutting-edge performance with its Ultra Control Technology, setting a new standard in robotic pool cleaning. It features the industry's most responsive app remote control, complete with a small rocket icon on the Beatbot App that activates a high-speed mode. This high-sensitivity, fast-moving skimmer is perfect for quick spot cleaning when time is tight. 

The app also allows you to schedule regular cleaning tasks and manage the dispensing of clarifying agents with ease. Its groundbreaking ClearWater Clarification system uses eco-friendly natural clarifiers made from recycled crab shells, all managed effortlessly through the app.

For added convenience, you can pause the cleaning process and return the robot to the poolside with a single '1-click come back' button. The app provides real-time updates on the robot’s status, cleaning history, water and air temperatures, and clarifying agent usages. Additionally, the iSkim Ultra features voice broadcast functionality, delivering real-time notifications about the robot's progress and any issues it encounters. 

Enjoy the pool without the maintenance 

We all stay so busy with work, social lives, travel, and family care, but with the ultra cleaning and control performance of the Beatbot iSkim Ultra, you can rely on Beatbot to keep your pool ready—giving you more time to enjoy your pool and less time spent on upkeep. The Beatbot iSkim Ultra, which is priced at $1499(MSRP), is available for pre-order now from Beatbot’s official website, starting from August 1 with a special early-bird discount. 

Grab a book, don your favorite swimsuit, and kick back in a lounger poolside while the Beatbot iSkim Ultra handles your pool maintenance dirty work.
