New US CIO appointments, December 2024

Movers & Shakers is where you can keep up with new CIO appointments and gain valuable insight into the job market and CIO hiring trends. As every company becomes a technology company, CEOs and corporate boards are seeking multi-dimensional CIOs and IT leaders with superior skills in technology, communications, business strategy, and digital innovation. The role is more challenging than ever before — but even more exciting and rewarding! If you have CIO job news to share, please email me!

Cencora names Pawan Verma CDIO

Cencora

Cencora, Inc. is a global pharmaceutical company that delivers pharmaceuticals, healthcare products, and solutions to care providers, and has a global workforce of over 46,000.

Verma joined from MetLife, where he most recently served as EVP and global CIO, overseeing an organization of 10,000, and spearheading the company’s differentiated digital experience. Prior to that, he served as CIO and customer experience officer at Foot Locker.

Ryan Smith named CDIO at Intermountain Health

Intermountain Health

Headquartered in Utah, Intermountain Health is a nonprofit system of 34 hospitals, approximately 400 clinics, medical groups with some 4,600 employed physicians and advanced care providers, a health plans division called Select Health with more than one million members, and other health services. Smith served in several senior leadership roles with Intermountain Health for nearly 20 years. He then held executive roles with Health Catalyst and served as SVP of technology and CIO at Banner Health. Following those positions, Smith returned to Intermountain as CIO from 2020 to 2022 before joining Graphite Health as its COO, and most recently served as Graphite’s interim president and CEO. He earned a BS from the University of Utah and an MBA from Western Governors University.

Guy Peri appointed CIDO of McCormick

McCormick

McCormick manufactures, markets, and distributes spices, seasoning mixes, condiments, and other products to the entire food industry, including e-commerce channels, grocery, food manufacturers, and foodservice businesses. Peri served across various leadership roles at Procter & Gamble for nearly 30 years, most recently serving as SVP and CIO of the company’s global healthcare unit. He holds a BS and MBA from the University of South Florida.

Michael Kempe joins Grant Thornton as CIO

Grant Thornton

Grant Thornton is the brand for two professional-services entities: Grant Thornton LLP, a licensed, certified public accounting (CPA) firm that provides audit and assurance services, and Grant Thornton Advisors LLC (not a licensed CPA firm), which exclusively provides non-attest offerings, including tax and advisory services. Prior to his new role with Grant Thornton, Kempe spent 12 years at KPMG LLP in various senior technology leadership roles, including regional and international CIO. He holds a BBA from Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen, the Netherlands.

UT Health San Antonio names Michael Schnabel as CIO

UT Health San Antonio

UT Health San Antonio provides education, biomedical research, patient care and community service to San Antonio and the South Texas/Border Region. Schnabel joined UT Health San Antonio in 2013 and has served as the CISO and assistant VP of information security and operations for more than a decade. He’s served as interim VP and CIO since July 2024. Prior to joining UT Health San Antonio Schnabel served as information security officer for City of Hope National Medical Center. He holds a BS and an MBA from Western Governors University.

Trex Company welcomes Anand Kangala as CIO

Trex

Based in Winchester, Virginia, Trex Company, Inc. is a manufacturer of wood-alternative decking and deck railing. Most recently, Kangala served as senior director at Home Depot, where he launched a transformation management office and led the digital strategy to build the foundation for interconnected retail vision through advanced technology and data-driven insights. Before Home Depot, he held various leadership roles in business and technology transformation at Edward Jones, Carter’s Inc., Cato Corporation, and IBM. He has a doctorate in business administration from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and an MBA from East Carolina University.

Mike Shelton appointed CIO at The University of Alabama

University of Alabama

Established in 1820 and opened to students in 1831, The University of Alabama (UA) is a public research university in Tuscaloosa. Shelton will move into his new role after serving as deputy CIO for the UA Office of IT since 2019. He has more than 25 years of experience in IT and started as a programmer at UA in 2006. In addition to his role with OIT, Shelton serves as an adjunct instructor teaching leadership and ethics in the Culverhouse College of Business. He earned a BS and an MA from The University of Alabama.

Newfold Digital appoints Tony Murphy as CIO

Newfold Digital

Established in 2021, Newfold Digital is a web and commerce technology company serving nearly seven million customers globally. Murphy joins Newfold with more than 30 years of experience in IT and strategic leadership, most recently serving as the CIO of Desktop Metal. Before that, he served as the CIO and CISO of Progress Software. Murphy has a BA from Maynooth University in Ireland and an MBA from Bentley University.

Michael Skaff joins Sequoia Living as CIO

Sequoia Living

Founded in 1958, Sequoia Living is a Bay Area nonprofit that provides homes and services that enrich the lives of older adults from all economic backgrounds. Prior to joining Sequoia Living, Skaff was VP of healthcare technology at Vayyar Imaging. He held previous leadership roles at Dele Health Tech and the Jewish Senior Living Group. Skaff holds a BA from the University of California, Davis.

Christopher Campise named CIO of Five Star Bank

Five Star Bank

Five Star Bank provides consumer and commercial banking and lending services to individuals, municipalities and businesses through banking locations spanning Western and Central NY and a commercial loan production office serving the Mid-Atlantic region. Campise joins Five Star from Delaware North Companies, where he served as senior director of enterprise architecture since 2021. Prior to that, he helped lead enterprise architecture during his seven year tenure at Highmark Blue Cross Blue Shield and spent nine years with the University at Buffalo. Campise earned a BS from Canisius College.

Kindeva Drug Delivery appoints Prakash Pandian as CIO

Kindeva

Kindeva Drug Delivery is a contract development and manufacturing organization

for sterile injectables, and pulmonary, nasal, transdermal, and intradermal finished doses. They operate manufacturing, research, and development facilities across the US and UK. Most recently, Pandian served as the global CIO at Curia, where he drove digital innovation and led the global IT and global supply chain functions. Prior to that, he held global leadership roles at Biogen, Millipore Corporation, and Merck KGaA. Prakash holds a BS from Sacred Heart College and a postgraduate diploma from Madras Christian College.

Damian Laviolette promoted to CIO at OnPoint Community Credit Union

OnPoint

OnPoint Community Credit Union, founded in 1932, is located in Oregon and serves over 576,000 members. Laviolette has worked at OnPoint since 2022. He previously served as VP and CISO, and before joining OnPoint, he served as SVP and CISO at Webster Bank and VP of Network Security Operations at Umpqua Bank. He holds an MS from Western Governors University.
