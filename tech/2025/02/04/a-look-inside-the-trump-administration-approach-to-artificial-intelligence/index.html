A look inside the Trump administration approach to artificial intelligence

President Trump rescinded his predecessor’s executive orders on artificial intelligence. That has implications for both government and industrial use of AI. At the same time he brought three tech moguls to the white house to announce a privately funded AI infrastructure. The head of AI policy at the Abundance Institute, Neil Chilson, joinsÂ the Federal Drive with Tom Temin with more analysis.
