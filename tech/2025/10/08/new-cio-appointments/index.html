New US CIO appointments, October 2025

Movers & Shakers is where you can keep up with new CIO appointments and gain valuable insight into the job market and CIO hiring trends. As every company becomes a technology company, CEOs and corporate boards are seeking multi-dimensional CIOs and IT leaders with superior skills in technology, communications, business strategy, and digital innovation. The role is more challenging than ever before — but even more exciting and rewarding! If you have CIO job news to share, please email me!

State Farm names Joe Park as CDIO

State Farm

State Farm and its affiliates are providers of auto and home insurance in the US, serving over 91 million policies and accounts including auto, fire, life, health, commercial policies, and financial services accounts. Park joins State Farm from Yum! Brands, where he was CDTO and president of Byte by Yum! Prior to Yum!, he held senior tech roles at Walmart, including VP of associate digital experience and enterprise architecture. He holds a BS from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute and an MBA from Northwestern’s Kellogg School of Management.

Steve Bronson announced as CIO for Southern Glazer’s Wine & Spirits

Southern Glazer’s

Southern Glazer’s Wine & Spirits is a worldwide distributor of beverage alcohol. The multi-generational, family-owned company has operations in 47 US markets and Canada, as well as brokerage operations through its Southern Glazer’s Travel Retail Sales & Export Division in the Caribbean, and Central and South America. Most recently, Bronson served as SVP of global technology infrastructure and operations at McDonald’s Corporation, where he spearheaded the company’s global tech transformation. Prior to McDonald’s, he held senior tech leadership roles at CDK Global, Nike, and Anheuser-Busch InBev.

Bridge Specialty Group appoints Steve Emmons as CIO

Bridge Specialty Group

Bridge Specialty Group, the wholesale division of Brown & Brown, specializes in all areas of property, casualty, and professional lines insurance, and Emmons most recently served as head of IT strategy and architecture. He was previously director of corporate services – insurance at BB&T Insurance Holdings among other roles with BB&T. He has a BS from Clarkson University.

Dawn-Marie Hutchinson joins Reynolds American as CIO

Reynolds American

Reynolds American Inc. is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the global BAT Group and the US parent company of R.J. Reynolds Tobacco Co., Santa Fe Natural Tobacco Co., American Snuff Company, R.J. Reynolds Vapor Co., and Modoral Brands. Hutchinson was most recently CISO for BAT Group. Prior to BAT, she was CISO of pharmaceuticals, R&D, and supply chain at GSK, and earlier as CISO at Urban Outfitters Group. She has a BA from the University of New Hampshire and an MBA from Saint Joseph’s University in Philadelphia.

Amway welcomes Ryan Talbott as CTO

Amway

Amway is an entrepreneur-led health and wellbeing company that empowers people to build their own Amway business, and helps people live better lives across more than 100 countries and territories. Prior to Amway, Talbott was VP and global CIO for BorgWarner, Inc., where he led high-performing tech teams to grow, improve, and sustain global business operations. He’s also held executive roles at Altimetrik, Fiat Chrysler Automobiles, and Accenture. He has a BBA from the University of Illinois and an MBA from Michigan State University.

Randy Dougherty promoted to CIO for Trellix

Trellix

Trellix provides hardware, software, and services to investigate cybersecurity attacks, protect against malicious software, and analyze IT security risks. Dougherty joined Trellix in 2022 and most recently served as VP, global infrastructure operations and strategic sourcing. Before Trellix, Dougherty served as VP of global IT at eXp Realty, and VP of global technology at The Nielsen Company. Dougherty earned a BS and an MBA from the University of Miami. 

Shayne Mehringer joins Redwood Services as CIO

Redwood Services

Founded in 2020, Memphis-based Redwood Services is building a family of people-focused essential home service companies, actively investing in the HVAC, plumbing, and electrical trades throughout the US. Mehringer most recently served as CIO for Premium Service Brands. Prior to that, he was CIO at Propelled Brands and Neighborly.

Kratos promotes Brian Shepard to CIO

Kratos

Kratos Defense & Security Solutions is a technology, products, systems, and software company addressing the defense, national security, and commercial markets. Shepard was most recently VP of IT where he led the growth and maturation of Kratos’ IT organization. Before, he was director of infrastructure of Haverstick Consulting, which was acquired by Kratos in 2006. Before that, he served four years in the US Marine Corps, and earned a BS from Marymount U.

Ravi Soin named CIO and CISO for Smartsheet

Smartsheet

Founded in 2005, Smartsheet is a cloud-based platform that allows organizations of all sizes to plan, capture, manage, automate, and report on work across the business. Soin joins from Edifecs where he was CIO and CISO. Before, Soin held various product roles at Microsoft and holds a BS and MS from USC, and an MBA from UW Foster School of Business.

Infoblox appoints Justin Kappers as CIO

Infoblox

Infoblox helps customers proactively see who and what connects to their network. Kappers joins from NetApp where he was VP of enterprise applications, and led a global team to modernize core business systems, including CRM, CPQ, and supporting subscription management platforms. Before NetApp, he held leadership positions at Broadcom, where he established the Enterprise Program Management Office and was IT chief of staff. He has a BS from Miami U.

Manu Narayan named CIO for GitLab

GitLab

GitLab is a DevSecOps platform for software innovation that enables organizations to increase developer productivity, improve operational efficiency, reduce security and compliance risk, and accelerate digital transformation. Before joining GitLab, Narayan served as VP of business technology at Confluent, driving the organization’s consumption-based transformation, internal AI initiatives, and many critical systems and process programs focused on long-term efficiency and scalability. Before, he led the internal tech teams at BlackLine, Applied Minds, and CIM.

Boomi appoints Keyur Ajmera as CIO

Boomi

Software company Boomi specializes in integration platform as a service (iPaaS), API management, master data management, and data preparation. Ajmera was most recently SVP of technology operations and innovation at Towne Park, and previously as CIO of iCIMS. He’s held senior roles at AppDynamics, PwC, INTTRA, and Deutsche Bank. Ajmera earned a BE from the University of Mumbai and an MS from San Francisco State University.

Eric Skinner promoted to CIO for Citadel Credit Union

Citadel Credit Union

Citadel Credit Union is a nonprofit, member-owned financial institution that provides banking, investments, and insurance services to more than 270,000 members. Skinner joined in 2013 and was most recently VP of IT operations and development, where he oversaw initiatives across business technologies, telecomms, infrastructure, and cybersecurity. He holds a BS from The University of Georgia.

CONA Services appoints Francesco Quinterno as CIO

CONA

CONA Services is the central IT provider for North American Coca-Cola bottlers, delivering business processes and digital solutions tailored to manufacturing and customer needs. Quinterno was most recently CTO, where he drove IT transformation, led teams through digital initiatives, and optimized tech infrastructure. He co-founded accessnow USA and held executive IT roles at Coca-Cola Enterprises, Inc., Warner Bros. Entertainment, and Colgate-Palmolive. He has a BSc. Eng from the University of the Witwatersrand.
