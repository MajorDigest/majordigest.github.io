Prime Big Deal Days FAQs

When does Prime Day end?

Amazon's October Prime Day sale, officially named Prime Big Deal Days, kicked off yesterday and ends tonight at 11:59 PM PDT.

That means that you have hours left to shop today's Prime Day deals. If you haven't already snagged a bargain, I've listed the best deals still available further down the page.

When do Prime Day deals end?

If you're wondering if all Prime Day deals end when the sale does (tonight at 11:59 PM PDT), the answer is a bit complicated. Most deals will likely end when Prime Day does. I've covered the sale for eight years now, and the best Prime Day deals will expire when the sale ends.

The next time you'll see discounts like this will be at one of Amazon's early Black Friday sale events or on Black Friday itself (November 28).

Do you need a Prime membership to shop Amazon's October sale?

Just like Amazon's July Prime Day sale, you need a Prime membership to shop today's Prime Day sale. Luckily, if you aren't already a member, you can sign up for a free 30-day trial right now, which gets you access to Amazon's event.
