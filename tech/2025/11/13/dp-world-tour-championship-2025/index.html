The DP World Tour Championship 2025 live streams bring an end to the European Tour golf season in fascinating fashion. Rory McIlroy is looking to end the year atop the rankings but will face stiff competition from Englishmen Marco Penge and Tyrrell Hatton, while Shane Lowry will also be teeing it up on the Earth Course at Jumeirah Golf Estates, Dubai.

It would take a spectacular blow-up from McIlroy were the Ulsterman not to pick up a fifth Race to Dubai title, but the 36-year-old will be determined to end a positive 2025 season on a high after completing the major set at the Masters and helping Europe win the Ryder Cup. McIlroy is ahead of English duo Penge and Hatton and will partner the former for Round 1 on Thursday. The tee-off times are in Race to Dubai ranking order.

Tommy Fleetwood, Ludvig Aberg, Robert McIntyre, Matt Fitzpatrick and Shane Lowry join McIlroy and Hatton among the victorious European Ryder Cup contingent seeking a victory, while Alex Noren, the Hojgaard twins, Patrick Reed, Michael Kim and Tom McKibbin are among a stacked field.

To add an extra level of intrigue, Fleetwood is paired with Aaron Rai, who beat him in a playoff for the Abu Dhabi HSBC Championship last week.

Can I watch DP World Tour Championship 2025 live streams for free?

Yes, in many countries in the world, thanks to the DP World Tour providing live coverage of certain groups of its events on the DP World Tour YouTube channel.

You'll be able to watch up to two Featured Groups per round make their way around the course, but not the all-encompassing coverage provided by dedicated broadcasters such as the Golf Channel in the US or Sky Sports in the UK. For that, you'll need a subscription.

The DP World Tour YouTube coverage is available globally, excluding the following countries: Argentina, Bolivia, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Costa Rica, Ecuador, El Salvador, Guatemala, Guyana, Honduras, Mexico, Nicaragua, Panama, Paraguay, Peru, St. Helena, Uruguay, Venezuela, Norway, Sweden, Denmark, Finland and Japan.
