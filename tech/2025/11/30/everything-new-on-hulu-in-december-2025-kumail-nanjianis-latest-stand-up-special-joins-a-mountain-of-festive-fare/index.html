Everything new on Hulu in December 2025 – Kumail Nanjiani's latest stand-up special joins a mountain of festive fare

We're now firmly into holiday movie season, and Hulu is bringing two of the biggest festive film franchises to their platform this month.

The series in question are Home Alone and The Santa Clause, and while the quality of the latter entries in the respective series may vary, we can all agree that there's some bonafide holiday classics among them. With both arriving on Decemeber 1, that's a total of eight Christmas crackers to see you through your yuletide binge.

If you're after more festive viewing, there's also The Wonderful World of Disney: Holiday Spectacular and CMA Country Christmas, so you won't go wanting for something to watch with your eggnog this season, and if you'd sooner escape the festivities, then Kumail Nanjiani’s first stand-up special in a decade, Night Thoughts, could be just the ticket.
