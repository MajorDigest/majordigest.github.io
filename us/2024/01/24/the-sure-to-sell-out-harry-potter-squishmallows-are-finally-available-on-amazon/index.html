Grab your galleons and order the long-awaited ‘Harry Potter’ Squishmallows now

As if Squishmallows weren’t already cute and collectible enough! The brand that brings kids (and, let’s be honest, plenty of grownups, too) huggable, pillowy stuffed animals has a new collection straight out of Hogwarts: “Harry Potter” Squishmallows made in the likenesses of some of your favorite characters from the magical books and movies.

Starting Jan. 25, you’ll be able to purchase Squishmallows of Harry and Ron, and Hermione will be available Feb. 13 — but they’re all available to preorder now. Just make sure you move quickly, because these adorable plushes are already on Amazon’s bestseller list. There’s a Hedwig plush, too, but it’s already sold out.
