Tween parents, hide your wallets: A Stanley Olivia Rodrigo collab is here

BestReviews is reader-supported and may earn an affiliate commission. Details.

Two things tweens love: Stanley Quenchers and Olivia Rodrigo

Does your tween feel like a “Vampire” on your bank account? Things may be about to get worse. Olivia Rodrigo, the Grammy-winning pop star, Gen Z icon and “Vampire” hitmaker, just announced a collaboration with Stanley (another thing tweens simply can’t get enough of). An Olivia-themed Stanley Quencher Tumbler is coming, and if you have a child between the ages of about 10 and 15, you might want to prepare yourself for this.

In case you’re not in the know, Rodrigo shot to stardom when she appeared on the Disney Channel series “Bizaardvark” and later had a starring role on the Disney+ series “High School Musical: The Musical: The Series.” But she’s now much better known for her powerhouse career as a solo pop artist. Her debut single, “Drivers License,” hit No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 and was the first song to reach more than a billion streams in 2021. She followed that up by winning a bunch of Grammys for her first album, “Sour.” Her new album, “Guts,” is also performing well.

Shop this article: Stanley Olivia Rodrigo 40 oz Quencher H2.0 FlowState Tumbler, Stanley 40 oz Stainless Steel H2.0 FlowState Quencher Tumbler in Mauve Shimmer, Stanley 40 oz Stainless Steel H2.0 FlowState Quencher Tumbler in Glazed Donut

Stanley tumblers are the TikTok-viral water bottles that tweens and teens love. They’re huge (you can get them in sizes up to 40 ounces). They have double-walled vacuum insulation that keeps drinks at temperature for hours. They come in a seriously enormous range of colors that gives kids a way to express themselves through their drinking vessels. The Stanley X Olivia Quencher will be no exception.

You can get the Stanley X Olivia Quencher tomorrow — but you’ll have to act fast

Stanley Olivia Rodrigo 40 oz Quencher H2.0 FlowState Tumbler

Olivia’s Quencher collab comes in her signature purple shade, and it has a clear “Sour” grape lid and reusable straw. The sides of the tumbler are decorated with stars in vampire-red and silver as a nod to the “Guts” album. This 40-ounce tumbler will be available for $55 starting Tuesday, July 9. To give buyers a fair chance, Stanley is running an EQL launch, meaning everyone who signs up during the drop window will have an equal chance of getting selected to purchase one, regardless of when they sign up.
