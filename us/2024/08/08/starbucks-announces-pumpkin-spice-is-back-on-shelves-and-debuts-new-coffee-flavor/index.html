Starbucks just dropped a brand new ‘Smoked Butterscotch’ coffee flavor for fall

As much as we all love to stop at Starbucks for a little treat (particularly in the fall, when the coffee chain rolls out its best menu of the year, hands down), that can really add up in price. Starbucks drinks are a splurge, not an everyday expense. So when you need your daily pick-me-up, how about a fall-inspired drink from Starbucks’ flavored coffees available in grocery stores?

Making coffee at home is the most affordable way to have a daily caffeinated treat. And Starbucks has a wide variety of flavored coffees that it sells in grocery stores across the country, including fall flavors that just returned. This year, Pumpkin Spice-flavored coffee products are back of course, since they’re a staple of the Starbucks fall lineup. But the brand also announced a brand new fall-flavored coffee this year that’s piquing our interest in a big way: Smoked Butterscotch, described as having “rich notes of salted brown butter and caramelized sugar.” We’re definitely going to have to try this.

In this article: Starbucks Ground Coffee, Smoked Butterscotch Naturally Flavored Coffee, 100% Arabica, Starbucks K-Cup Coffee Pods, Smoked Butterscotch Naturally Flavored Coffee for Keurig Coffee Makers, 100% Arabica and Starbucks K-Cup Coffee Pods—Pumpkin Spice Flavored Coffee—100% Arabica

If you’re also interested in trying Smoked Butterscotch coffee — or any of Starbucks’ other fall flavors — they’re available now, before the fall menu actually arrives at Starbucks locations. You can find them in grocery stores or online, so we rounded them up below to make your shopping experience a little faster and easier. Enjoy your little treats this fall!
