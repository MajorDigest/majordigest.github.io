LEGO’s popular Advent calendars are already selling out fast

The holiday season probably feels like it’s too far away to start thinking about. But if you want to get your hands on any of this year’s must-have gifts, now is the time to start planning — and shopping. One gift that flies off store shelves year after year is Advent calendars from LEGO. The 2024 calendars have arrived, and they’re already selling quickly. That means that if you have a LEGO lover in your life who would like to unbox buildable gifts in the days leading up to Christmas, it’s time to buy an Advent calendar while you still can.

This year’s LEGO Advent calendars include fan favorites like LEGO Star Wars, LEGO City, LEGO Harry Potter, LEGO Disney and more. They all come with a wide variety of minifigures and small, buildable surprises, making them the perfect gift for those who just can’t get enough LEGO in their lives. The hardest part for gift-givers is choosing which one to buy — especially since you’ll have to decide quickly; these calendars are already selling thousands of times and they just launched. They definitely won’t last long.

In this article: LEGO Star Wars 2024 Advent Calendar for Kids, LEGO City 2024 Advent Calendar and LEGO Harry Potter 2024 Advent Calendar

So if a LEGO Advent calendar is on your holiday shopping list, consider the shopping season to be starting. Sure, back-to-school season has barely wrapped up, and fall hasn’t even officially arrived yet. But if you want to grab one of these calendars before they sell out, now is your chance.
